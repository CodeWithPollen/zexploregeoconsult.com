What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive geotechnical solutions tailored to the unique demands of your project — from initial investigation to final design.

Geotechnical Investigation

Thorough subsurface exploration using advanced drilling, sampling, and in-situ testing techniques to characterize site conditions accurately before construction begins.

Soil Testing

Laboratory and field testing of soil samples including bearing capacity, consolidation, permeability, and shear strength analysis to ensure structural safety.

Foundation Design

Expert design of shallow and deep foundation systems including footings, rafts, piles, and retaining structures tailored to your site's geotechnical profile.

Geological Surveys

Detailed mapping and assessment of geological formations, fault lines, and rock quality to support infrastructure, mining, and environmental projects.
